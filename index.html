A Bit About Me

Here's the short version. I'm Becky — a Yorkshire lass with a professional microphone and a genuine love for storytelling. I trained as a voice actor with BRAVA, but honestly, my obsession with audio started long before that.

I spent years producing at BBC Radio 1, working alongside the likes of Nick Grimshaw, Jo Whiley, and Sara Cox. It was brilliant, chaotic, and taught me everything about what makes audio connect with people. That experience is in every read I do — I understand what producers need, what editors are listening for, and how to make a script come alive.

These days, I work from my home studio delivering broadcast-quality audio with a fast turnaround. I'm also a podcaster, a marketing manager, and a mum who somehow fits all of this in between school runs and approximately forty cups of tea a day. My voice is warm, authentic, and unmistakably northern — and if your project needs someone who sounds like an actual person rather than a polished corporate machine, we should probably have a chat.
